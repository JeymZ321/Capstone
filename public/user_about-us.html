Our History


Reynaldo's Car Care is a family-owned business, legally registered under the Cajeda Family, located at 34 Sumulong Highway, Mayamot, Antipolo City, Rizal. Established on July 17, 2017, the business operates with a team of thirteen employees. Occupying a 600-square-meter rented space, it can service up to 26 cars, with demand often exceeding capacity.The business evolved from the owners' experience in mechanical repairs and gradually expanded into car maintenance.

Our Mission


The mission of Reynaldo's Car Care is to provide high quality, fast and zero back job comprehensive auto body repair. We focus onn 100% customer satisfaction, friendlinesss, and time to completion to discover new ways and trends to exceed customer satisfaction.
